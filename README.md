# Boardoza Phoenix24 Breakout Board

The **Boardoza Phoenix24** is a high-performance **addressable RGB (ARGB) LED breakout board** featuring **24 individually addressable LEDs** based on the **T3A33BRG-H9C0002X1U1930** LED chipset. It is designed to deliver vibrant, dynamic lighting effects while maintaining reliable signal integrity across complex LED chains.

Thanks to its **DIN, CIN, DOUT, and COUT** signal routing and support for **both CMOS and TTL logic levels**, Phoenix24 can be seamlessly integrated into a wide range of embedded systems. With **THT (Through-Hole)** and **SMD (Surface-Mount)** connection options, the board is suitable for both rapid prototyping and final product designs, making it ideal for **lighting installations, user interfaces, visual feedback systems, and decorative applications**.

## Key Features

- **24 Addressable RGB LEDs:** Enables vivid, dynamic, and individually controllable lighting effects.
- **CMOS & TTL Signal Compatibility:** Ensures seamless integration with a wide range of microcontrollers and logic systems.
- **Dedicated Data & Clock Lines:** Separate DIN, CIN, DOUT, and COUT signals provide reliable signal flow and easy daisy-chaining.
- **Flexible Connection Options:** Supports both THT (Through-Hole) and SMD (Surface-Mount) connections for versatile assembly.
- **Wide Application Support:** Ideal for lighting systems, visual feedback, user interfaces, and decorative projects.

## Technical Specifications

**Model:** Phoenix24  
**Manufacturer:** Boardoza  
**Functions:** Addressable RGB LED Lighting Board  
**Input Voltage:** 3.3 V / 5 V  
**LED Model:** T3A33BRG-H9C0002X1U1930  
**Number of LEDs:** 24   
**LED Type:** Individually addressable RGB LEDs  
**Logic Compatibility:** TTL & CMOS  
**Data Interface:** Serial Data & Clock (DIN / CIN / DOUT / COUT)  
**Daisy-Chain Support:** Yes  
**Operating Temperature:** -40 °C to +85 °C   
**Board Dimensions:** 67.24 mm × 67.18 mm
